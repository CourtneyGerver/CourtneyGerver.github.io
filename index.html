Courtney Gerver, Ph.D.

I am a researcher with 9+ years of experience leveraging a diverse set of qualitative and quantitative research methodologies. In my current role as a user experience researcher, I use this expertise to gather actionable insights and translate them into user-centric product decisions.

Over my tenure, I’ve had the ability to work on a wide range of projects. I’ve applied data-driven, design-thinking principles to projects scaling from mobile applications to large-scale websites. I’ve also generated insights from participants ranging from 6-94, which have included individuals from the general population to specialty, harder-to-recruit professions.

With an academic background in cognitive psychology, especially as it relates to older adults aged 65+, I am uniquely equipped with a deep understanding of memory, creativity, attention, and problem-solving. This understanding has equipped me with the tools to understand user behaviors, motivations, and pain points.

I am committed to accessible research and design and actively participate in opportunities to advance product usage for all. When not aiming to push the boundaries of user research, I enjoy curling, hiking, and baking.
